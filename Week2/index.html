I am currently completing an undergraduate degree in Information Technology from the University of Technology Sydney (UTS). I am majoring in Business Information Systems Management and Data Analytics.

I was born in Mohali, a commercial hub lying in Punjab (state in the northern side of India).

I love playing cricket, exploring scenic places and trying out food from differing cultures!

My dream job would be to develop programs for a thriving company, alongside a highly skilled, multidisciplinary team. I would love to work in a position in which I can be creative and learn something new every single day.

I am very passionate about new trends in technology. The idea of a federal election being securely conducted using blockchain or surgeons observing a patient's spine in 3D using augmented reality glasses has a great appeal to me.

I am learning web development because:

  • Finding solutions to challenging problems gives me a thrill. I love solving sudokus, puzzles, crosswords etc.
  • There will always be something new to learn as updates to web development tools are made very rapidly. I want to stay in that state of learning as long as I possibly can.
  • Web development brings out your creativity.
